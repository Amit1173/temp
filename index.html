About Me

Lighting Artist & Animator

I consider myself to have a strong technical and artistic cinematography background. I am very fond of Lighting and addressing the challenges. I love what lighting brings to the story telling and how it evokes the emotions and supports the narrative and i also like the limitations and challenges CG brings on a daily basis. Finding a solution to a problem or a creative way to get a better results is extremely rewarding. As an CGI artist knowing the rules for creating the illusion of the shape, evoking an emotional response and creating visual interest in a composition gives me satisfaction.
